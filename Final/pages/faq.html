GeneralQuestions

  • How do I receive the macaron pyramids?

    If you order the pyramids online, the macarons come in protective trays, and you will receive the tiers to assemble them. You can find a how-to video here. If you pick up the pyramid at a location, they can assemble it for you - usually for an extra cost. The small pyramid brings 40 macarons and 4 layers; the medium pyramid brings 75 macarons and 6 layers; the large pyramid brings 200 macarons and 10 layers.

  • Where are you located?

    To find your nearest Woops! locations, visit our locations page. You can also find us at pop-up events throughout the year and during the holiday season at your local holiday markets.

  • How and where are the macarons made?

    Our macarons are crafted with love at our New York bakery located at 435 Longfellow Ave, Bronx, NY 10474. Our master pastry chef created our 20-plus flavors from an Old World recipe that leaves us plenty of room to innovate. Every macaron is handmade using only the finest ingredients.

Shipping & Delivery

  • Do you deliver?

    We do deliver! You can find a list of our locations with their phone numbers here, call them to coordinate delivery.

  • Can I return an item?

    Unfortunately, we do not accept returns of our products. However, if you are unsatisfied with your purchase for any reason, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We would love to assist in turning the situation around!

  • What if my package arrives damaged?

    We take extra precaution to ensure that your macarons arrive in pristine condition. To achieve this, our macarons are placed in a protective clamshell and shipped chilled in special packaging. However, if your gift box or macarons do arrive broken, please let us know!

Mac-a-ron

A macaron or French macaroon is a sweet meringue-based confection made with egg white, icing sugar, granulated sugar, almond powder or ground almond, and food coloring.
